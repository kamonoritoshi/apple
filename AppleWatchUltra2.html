Apple Watch Ultra 2

From $799 or $66.58/mo. for 12 mo.*

Overview

The Apple Watch Ultra 2 has a luxurious titanium frame and a new generation S9 SiP chip, providing a stable and smooth user experience. The watch has three strap versions suitable for different user needs.

Key Features

  • Tough 49mm titanium case with customizable Action button strikes a balance with weight, ruggedness, and corrosion resistance
  • All-new S9 SiP powers our brightest display ever and a magical new way to use your watch without touching the screenGet deep insights into your physical and mental health including heart rate, mood, and more
  • The most accurate GPS in a sports watch in dense urban environments
  • Up to 36 hours of battery life, up to 72 hours on low power settings, and up to 17 hours of workout use in Low Power Mode
  • Pair with three band styles specialized for outdoor adventure, water sports, and all workout types
