Apple Watch Series 9

From $399 or $33.25/mo. for 12 mo.*

Overview

The Apple Watch Series 9 has an on-chip S9 SiP - a CPU with 5.6 billion transistors that helps deliver a performance improvement of more than 60% compared to the S8 generation. The device screen has a maximum brightness of 2000 nits, providing an outstanding display experience. Along with that, this Apple Watch s9 is also equipped with many features to support health monitoring and smart exercise.

Key Features

  • Brighter Always-On Retina display with a durable, crack-resistant front crystal
  • Get deep insights into your physical and mental health including heart rate, mood, and more
  • With advanced workout metrics and views
  • The all-new S9 SiP powers a magical new way to use your watch without touching the screen
  • Innovative safety features including Emergency SOS, Fall Detection, and Crash Detection
