Overview

The iPhone 15 Pro is equipped with a new 6.1-inch Dynamic Island screen with ProMotion technology and Always On Display feature. The device also has many changes in design with an action button and USB-C charging port, and a luxurious titanium frame. This new generation of iPhone 15 phones is equipped with 4 new colors such as natural titanium, black titanium, white titanium and blue titanium.

Key Features

  • 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display featuring ProMotion, Always-On, and Dynamic Island

  • 48MP Main camera for super-high-resolution photos and 3x Telephoto camera
