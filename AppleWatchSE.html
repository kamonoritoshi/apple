Apple Watch SE

From $249 or $20.75/mo. for 12 mo.*

Overview

Apple Watch SE 2023 is equipped with a Retina screen for extremely sharp display and extremely sturdy, luxurious and beautiful tempered glass. The watch size is a compact 44mm with a smooth rubber strap. The Apple Watch SE 2023 product is integrated with a powerful S8 SiP chip, with up to 18 hours of use.

Key Features

  • Call and message even when you don't have your iPhone nearby with GPS + Cellular models
  • Get advanced workout metrics and views
  • Track your sleep stages with the Sleep app, and check your heart rate anytime
  • Innovative safety features including Emergency SOS, Fall Detection, and Crash Detection
  • Family Setup lets you pair watches for family members without an iPhone
